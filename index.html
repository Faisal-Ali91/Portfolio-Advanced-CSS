About Me

My name is Faisal Sabar Ali and I live in Birmingham, United Kingdom. I have spent most of my life in Birmingham and have studied accounting but discovered it wasn't for me. I have worked in the civil service since 2016 and it dawned on me during the COVID-pandemic that I need to gain skills in an industry that I have a passion for. I have always been interested in coding and the world that it can create so I decided to enter this world.

I started my Coding-Bootcamp course in May 2021 and so far I have completed the first unit of HTML and advanced CSS and I am currently mid-way through my Javascript unit. I am currently studying intensively whilst working full-time and running a household. My dream is to get a job in the Software-Development world at the conclusion of this bootcamp or even during it. I believe that I am hard-working and have the ability to learn and then re-learn when more adequate information arises.
